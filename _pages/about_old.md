## About

My name is Ying Zhong(鍾穎）. I am an Assistant Professor at Tsukuba University of Technology in Japan. I am also a Visiting Researcher and a Part-time Lecturer at the University of Tsukuba.

My research focuses on accessibility and human-centered AI,
aiming to support everyday communication and safety for diverse users,
including deaf and hard-of-hearing people, blind and low vision people, elderly people and foreign residents.

I study human–AI collaboration, multimodal interaction design,
and AI-assisted communication systems, and apply them to real-world
service encounters, public events, and daily living environments.
Apart from research activities, My hobbies are cooking, music instruments(guitar and ukulele), ballet and movies.
