## About

I am an Assistant Professor at Tsukuba University of Technology in Japan. 

My research focuses on **Accessibility, Human-AI Interaction, and Immersive Learning Systems**.  
In particular, I study technologies that support communication and education for **Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing users**, including AI-assisted learning systems and VR-based training environments.

My current work explores how **AI and immersive technologies can support academic communication training for deaf learners**, such as presentation and interview practice.
